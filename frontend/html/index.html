What We Do

As a club founded in ethnic diversity, the Mixed Student Union aims to provide an inclusive environment for students whose rich cultural background is comprised of more than one ethnic identity. Often times, those who share this unique experience may struggle to find commonality among their peers, or even feel pressured to conform to cultural assumptions imposed on them by others due to the ambiguity of their skin complexion, facial features, hair styles, and other physical identifiers. The Mixed Student Union seeks to provide an open, safe, and inviting space where these topics can be discussed by those who share this experience.
